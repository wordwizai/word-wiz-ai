**Insight**: All competitors use fixed content libraries, no AI generation  
**Opportunity**: Highlight unlimited, personalized sentence generation via GPT-4  
**SEO Angle**: "AI-generated reading practice" / "personalized reading sentences"

### Gap 3: High Subscription Costs

**Insight**: Most require $10-20/mo subscriptions, no free options with core features  
**Opportunity**: Emphasize free tier with essential features (speech analysis)  
**SEO Angle**: "free reading app with speech recognition" / "affordable phonics app"

### Gap 4: App-Only or Web-Only Silos

**Insight**: Many are mobile-only (HOMER, Khan Kids) or primarily web (Starfall)  
**Opportunity**: True cross-platform web-first approach, no downloads needed  
**SEO Angle**: "no download reading app" / "browser-based phonics practice"

### Gap 5: No Teacher-Student Collaboration Features

**Insight**: Limited classroom management (only ABCmouse, Reading Eggs have school editions)  
**Opportunity**: Teacher dashboard with class management, student progress tracking  
**SEO Angle**: "teacher phonics dashboard" / "classroom reading management"

### Gap 6: Passive Learning Over Active Production

**Insight**: Most competitors focus on clicking, watching, multiple choice  
**Opportunity**: Active speaking and immediate phoneme-level correction  
**SEO Angle**: "active reading practice" / "speak and learn phonics"
